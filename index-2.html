About TrueHelp

We hire maids, drivers, cooks and trust them to take care of us and our loved ones. But, do we know who they really are? Do we have their real employment history? Their address verified? Or, most importantly, do we know if they have any criminal history?

Meet GetTrueHelp! India's first complete, comprehensive background verification and work history service for our hard worker friends. Be safe than sorry! Whether you want to hire a maid, a driver, a part time gardener or just about anybody for your home or office, we can help you get that peace of mind by getting them vetted. Aadhar verification - done! Last employer feedback - yes! Criminal history - check!

Best of all, once you enroll your employee in GetTrueHelp, we can register them with your local police station, which, BTW, is required by the Indian law. So what are you waiting for? Letsssss GetTrueHelp!
