About TrueHelp

Did you know? 40% thefts in India are committed by domestic helpers? Police verification is mandatory for domestic workers with legal implications if you don't comply? But don't worry, TrueHelp truly helps!

TrueHelp is India's first ML/AI driven complete, comprehensive and continuous verification™ service for employees. Be safe than sorry and know your employee! Whether you are a Business who wants to get your employees background verification done or an Individual who wants to hire a maid, a driver, a part time gardener or just about anybody for your home or office - we can help you get that peace of mind by getting them vetted.

Once you enroll your employees in TrueHelp, we will continuosly update you if we get any major information about them such as change in Aadhar address, new employer record, new criminal record etc. So what are you waiting for? Lets get TrueHelp!
